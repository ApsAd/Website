SSN College of Engineering has a faculty strength of 260 with a faculty student ratio of 1 faculty for every 15 students. Out of the total, 156 faculty are PhDs. and 91 are in the process of completing their PhD.s. Periodic guest lectures, workshops are organized for the benefit of students to supplement their curriculum. This facilitates our students to enrich their knowledge and to update the current industry trends.
